## Design & Aesthetic Instructions
**Core Vibe:** Modern, simple, minimalist, Google-like aesthetics. White background, highly legible.
**Typography:** Clean, modern sans-serif fonts (e.g., Inter, Roboto, Open Sans).
**Reference Images:** The user has saved screenshots of websites they like into the `Reference/` folder. 
**Action Required:** Review the inspiration screenshots in the `Reference/` folder. Pay close attention to the spacing, font weights, component layouts, and minimalistic visuals. Emulate this clean, white-page, modern aesthetic in your CSS implementation. Avoid cluttered designs or overly complex interactive elements; keep it straightforward and professional.
**Mobile Optimization:** Ensure the layout is fully responsive and looks perfect on mobile devices (e.g., using proper media queries, flexbox/grid for adaptable layouts, and accessible touch targets).
